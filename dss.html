How Scoring Works

Final Score = (Price × W1) + (Performance × W2) + (Camera × W3) + (Battery × W4) + (Display × W5) + (Software Support × W6)

Each phone has a criterion score from 0 to 10. A higher final score means a better overall match for the selected weights.
